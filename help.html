How can we help you?

Find answers to common questions about booking rides or listing your vehicle.

For Passengers

How do I book a seat?

Search for your route, find a driver traveling at your preferred time, and click the "Call" or "Chat" button on their card. You speak directly with the driver to confirm your seat and pickup point.

Do I have to pay online?

No. PahadiRide is just a directory. You pay the driver directly (via UPI or cash) when you board the vehicle.

Are the drivers verified?

Drivers with a green "Verified" badge have submitted a copy of their RC (Registration Certificate) and Driving License to our team for basic verification.

For Drivers

How much does it cost to list my vehicle?

Creating an account and listing your route on PahadiRide is currently 100% free.

What if my vehicle gets full?

Log in to your Driver Dashboard and flip the status switch to "OFFLINE" or update your available seats to zero. This stops passengers from calling you when you don't have space.

Our team is ready to assist you with any technical issues.
